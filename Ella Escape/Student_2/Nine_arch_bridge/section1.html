Nine Arch Bridge

Nine Arch Bridge, Ella Overview

Nine Arch Bridge is also known as The Bridge in the Sky one of the most visited tourist spots in the country and one of the top attractions in Sri Lanka. Located between Ella and Demodara stations, on a green valley, and surrounded by gorgeous tea plantations. This ancient construction spans 91 meters with a height of 24 meters and its nine magnificent arches make it a truly picturesque place. The view from the bridge is stunning, and it's a great place to take photos and enjoy the natural beauty of Sri Lanka. The Ella to Kandy train ride runs near the Nine Arch Bridge and is named one of the most beautiful train rides in the world. If you're planning a trip to Ella, be sure to add the Nine Arch Bridge to your itinerary!

History Of Nine Arch Bridge In Ella

The Nine Arch Bridge is located in Demodara, which is a small village near Ella in the Badulla district of Sri Lanka. The bridge was constructed during the British colonial period in Sri Lanka, which lasted from 1815 to 1948.The bridge was built during World War I, and due to the war effort, steel and other metals were in short supply. This scarcity of materials made it difficult and costly to use steel or other metals for the construction of the bridge. As a result, the engineers and workers involved in the construction of the Nine Arch Bridge had to rely on locally available materials like stones, cement, and bricks to build the bridge. The bridge was designed by a British architect named Harold Cuthbert Marwood and built by local. laborers using traditional construction techniques. The bridge has a unique design, with nine arches that were constructed using stones and bricks without any steel or concrete.The Nine Arch Bridge is a testament to the engineering and architectural prowess of both the British and Sri Lankan people, and it stands as a symbol of the country's rich history and cultural heritage.

How To Get To Nine Arch Bridge In Ella

The Nine Arch Bridge is located in the town of Ella in the Badulla district of Sri Lanka. The Nine Arch Bridge is located near the Demodara railway station, which is about 5 kilometers away from Ella town. Here are some ways to get to the Nine Arch Bridge.

By Train

Most popular way to reach the Nine Arch Bridge is by train. You can take a train from Colombo Fort to Ella, and the journey will take around 6-7 hours. The train ride is scenic, and you will get to see some of the stunning landscapes of Sri Lanka.

By Tuk Tuk

The easiest option is to get a tuk tuk from town, directly to the Nine Arch Bridge. The ride will take around 20 minutes, and it will cost you around 500-1000 LKR. To save time, ask them to drop you off near the tunnel and walk to the bridge the last piece.

By Foot

If you like hiking, you can also walk from the falls to the Nine Arch Bridge. The hike takes about 30-45 minutes and takes you through some of Sri Lanka's most beautiful tea plantations and forests. And the best option is to keep Google Maps open all the time.

Things To Know Before Visiting Nine Arch Bridge

Best time to visit

The best time to visit the Nine Arch Bridge is during the dry season from December to March when the weather is pleasant and the skies are clear.

Entrance fee

No entrance fee to visit the Nine Arch Bridge. However, if you're taking a guided tour or hiring a tuk-tuk, there may be additional charges.

Photography

It's important to be respectful of the local culture. Ask for permission before taking photos of locals, and avoid taking photos of religious sites without permission.

Dress code

Sri Lanka is a conservative country, so it's best to dress modestly when visiting the Nine Arch Bridge. Avoid wearing revealing clothing.

Guided Tours

You can easily visit Nine Arch Bridge independently, but if you have limited time in Ella and want to hit all the main attractions in one day, you can opt for a guided tour.

Accommodation

There are many accommodation options available near the Nine Arch Bridge, ranging from budget-friendly guesthouses to luxurious resorts.
