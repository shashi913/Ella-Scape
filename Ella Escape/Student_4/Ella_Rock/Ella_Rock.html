Ella Rock

Ella Rock Description

Ella Rock is a popular hiking destination in Ella, Sri Lanka, known for its stunning views of the surrounding countryside. It is a 1,041-meter high peak located about 8 km from the town of Ella.

The hike to Ella Rock usually takes about 2-3 hours, and the trail passes through tea plantations, small villages, and lush green forests. Along the way, hikers can enjoy stunning views of the surrounding hills, valleys, and waterfalls.

The hike is considered to be moderate to difficult, as it involves some steep climbs and rocky terrain. However, the effort is worth it when you reach the summit of Ella Rock, where you can enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

Best time to Hike Ella Rock

  • Climbing the mountain in rainy weather is dangerous. Because the way up the mountain is slippery during the rainy season and it is more dangerous in the dangerous hilly areas.

  • Best time to climb the mountain is in the morning or evening on a rainless day. Because it gets very hot in the afternoon and it is difficult to climb it at such a time.

  • But even late in the evening there is an ominous atmosphere. The trip along the Ella railway to the mountain is something that should be done in good weather. When a train is approaching, move safely out of the way.

How to climb Ella Rock

Step 1

you have set off from Ella train station and walk on the train track towards kithalella. When a train comes you have to move safely out of the railway track. you will be able to hear it long before arrives. After about 30-40 minutes you will see a yellow sign on the left saying 15Km/h then get off the train tracks and turn left into the fields.

Step 2

Second section of the hiking is the confusing part. There will be a small bridge after crossing it you will see a path that goes steeply uphill through tea bushes. Take that path and go through tea bushes and tall grass lands.

Step 3

The third section of the hike is more challenging physically, but the nature view will helps you to pass that challenge. Once you are out of the trees you will reach the top of the Ella Rock. Now sit back and enjoy the views.

Things you should know & Things you should borrow

Things you should know

  • Trail Difficulty: The hike to Ella Rock is considered to be moderate to difficult, so make sure you are physically prepared for the challenge. The trail involves steep climbs, rocky terrain, and can take around 2-3 hours to complete.
  • Weather: Sri Lanka's climate is generally hot and humid, so be prepared for the heat and humidity. It's recommended to start your hike early in the morning to avoid the midday heat. Additionally, check the weather forecast before starting your hike to avoid hiking during heavy rain.
  • Permits: There are no permits required to hike Ella Rock, but it's always best to check with local authorities before starting your hike to ensure there are no closures or restrictions in place.

Things you should borrow

  • Comfortable Hiking Shoes: A pair of sturdy and comfortable hiking shoes with good grip is essential for tackling the rocky terrain and steep climbs.
  • Water Bottle: Bring a reusable water bottle to stay hydrated during the hike.
  • Sun Protection: Don't forget to bring sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat to protect yourself from the sun.
  • Snacks: Pack some high-energy snacks such as granola bars, nuts, and dried fruit to keep you fueled throughout the hike.
  • Insect Repellent: There may be insects on the trail, so it's a good idea to bring insect repellent to keep them at bay.
